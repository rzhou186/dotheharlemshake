About

About the Project

Make the Harlem Shake! is a web application that builds your own Harlem Shake videos for you, bypassing the hassle of video editing with tools like Windows Movie Maker.

The web app uses your laptop's webcam to record the 2 video parts independently. It then processes the recordings and provides a finished download link, complete with music and effects. Please note that this processing can take some time.

About Us

We're a duo of Stanford undergraduates who love hacking and social coding. This was our most recent weekend project. Hope you enjoy it!
